scipy.special.itstruve0¶

scipy.special.itstruve0(x) = <ufunc 'itstruve0'>¶

Integral of the Struve function of order 0.

\[I = \int_0^x H_0(t)\,dt\]
Parameters:

x : array_like

Upper limit of integration (float).
Returns:

I : ndarray

The integral of \(H_0\) from 0 to x.

See also

struve

Notes

Wrapper for a Fortran routine created by Shanjie Zhang and Jianming Jin [R465].

References

[R465](1, 2) Zhang, Shanjie and Jin, Jianming. “Computation of Special Functions”, John Wiley and Sons, 1996. http://jin.ece.illinois.edu/specfunc.html
