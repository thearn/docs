scipy.special.besselpoly¶

scipy.special.besselpoly(a, lmb, nu) = <ufunc 'besselpoly'>¶

Weighted integral of a Bessel function.

\[\int_0^1 x^\lambda J_\nu(2 a x) \, dx\]

where \(J_\nu\) is a Bessel function and \(\lambda=lmb\), \(\nu=nu\).
