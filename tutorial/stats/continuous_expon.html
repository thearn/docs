\begin{eqnarray*} f\left(x\right) & = & e^{-x}\\ F\left(x\right) & = & \Gamma\left(1,x\right)=1-e^{-x}\\ G\left(q\right) & = & -\log\left(1-q\right)\end{eqnarray*}
